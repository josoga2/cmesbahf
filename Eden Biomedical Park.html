Biomedical Science & Engineering (BSE)

Our BSE group seeks to nurture outstanding innovations in mental and reproductive health.
One of our collaborations include the industrial design and engineering company, Crafts Design Ltd., Nigeria.

Life Sciences (LS)

Modern drug discovery in key areas affecting the Nigerian population: Type II Diabetes, heart diseases, antimicrobial resistance and Neglected tropical diseases (NTD).

Public Health

Our chain of innovations provide state of the art technologies for the ost important public health issues including mental health, NTDs, malaria and HIV. To answer the physician's questions, we incorporate data science and machine learning in all our discovery pipelines.
